What are mice?

Mice are small rodents that can be found both in the wild and as domesticated pets. Some common mice species are:

  1. The House Mouse (Mus musculus)
  2. The Deer Mouse (Peromyscus maniculatus)
  3. The White-footed Mouse (Peromyscus leucopus)

How much does a mouse drink in a day?

A mouse drinks around 5.8 mls of water per day, which is about 25% of an adult mouse's body weight.

What are yaks?

Yaks are a species of long-haired cattle. There are two types of yaks:

  1. The Domesticated Yak (Bos grunniens)
  2. The Wild Yak (Bos mutus)

How much does a yak drink in a day?

A yak drinks around 9 gallons of water per day, or a whopping 34,068.706 mls!

How much food does an average spoon hold?

A United States tablespoon holds approximately 14.8 ml, or 0.50 U.S. fl. oz.

Mice and Spoons

For a mouse to get an entire day's worth of water intake from a spoon, it would need less than one full spoon. 5.8 mls of water would be roughly 40% of a tablespoon's full 14.8 mls capacity.

Yaks and Spoons

For a yak to get an entire day's worth of water intake from a spoon, it would need nearly 2,302 spoons. That's a lot of spoons!
